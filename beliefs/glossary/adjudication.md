# adjudication

The written justification is what separates a maintainable [gold set](/beliefs/glossary/gold-set.md) from a bare data file: when a score is disputed or a [LLM-as-judge](/beliefs/glossary/llm-as-judge.md) drifts, the notes are what re-calibration stands on. This bundle's gold docs carry them per row, alongside band labels for cases whose right answer is deliberate-negative or undefined.
