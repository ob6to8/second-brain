# AI-native

Distinguished from an org that merely *uses* AI tools: an AI-native startup treats AI as the substitute for functions traditionally filled by headcount — research, coding, and operational workflow automation — collapsing the traditional validate → raise → hire → build cycle that assumed each growth phase required a bigger team and a fresh funding round. The founder's role shifts from individual contributor to orchestrator of agents.
