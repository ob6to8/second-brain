# assumption

A belief held provisionally rather than compelled by evidence — the natural
target when a contradiction must be repaired. Technical senses:

1. **JTMS (Doyle)** — a node whose supporting justification has a nonempty
   *outlist*: the belief rests partly on the *absence* of other beliefs, which
   is what makes it eligible for reasoned retraction.
2. **ATMS (de Kleer)** — the primitive data of the system: every other datum
   is labeled by the sets of assumptions
   ([environments](/beliefs/glossary/environment-atms.md)) it derives from.
3. **GSN** — an oval-'A' element presenting "an intentionally unsubstantiated
   statement" whose scope is the entire argument it annotates.

Distinct from a *premise*, which is believed unconditionally and is not a
candidate for retraction.
