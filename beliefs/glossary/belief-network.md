# belief network

A belief network's nodes are the stochastic (random) variables and its edges encode conditional dependence. Popularized by Judea Pearl, the "belief" is the graphical-models sense — a probabilistic dependency structure — with no connotation of confidence or opinion. A [deep belief network](/beliefs/glossary/deep-belief-network.md) is a deep, learned instance of this idea. (Contrast the epistemic-grounding graph of the brain's [epistemic overlay](/beliefs/glossary/epistemic-overlay.md), whose edges carry no probabilities.)
