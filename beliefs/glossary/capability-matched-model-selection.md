# capability-matched model selection

The standing direction that agents allocate model capability by the epistemic weight of the motion, not habit or availability: concentrate the strongest models where output becomes canonical, where a judgment is rendered, or where errors are silent, and delegate derivational, oracle-checked, and high-volume/low-stakes motions to cheaper tiers. It is the model-layer application of [orchestration](/beliefs/glossary/orchestration.md) — strong-model attention is spent the way the operator's own attention is. Deliberately a [doctrine](/beliefs/glossary/doctrine.md), not a policy: selection is a runtime act enforceable only through its [attribution shadow](/beliefs/glossary/attribution-shadow.md). Canonically defined in the [capability-matched model selection doctrine](/meta/doctrine/capability-matched-model-selection.md).
