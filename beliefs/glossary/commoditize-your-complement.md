# commoditize your complement

Classic instances: OS vendors commoditizing hardware, Google funding browsers.
In the agent tooling market it runs in both directions at once — model labs
push open protocols like the
[Model Context Protocol](/beliefs/glossary/model-context-protocol.md) to
commoditize agent *surfaces* and sell intelligence, while AI-IDE vendors push
closed integration to differentiate the surface and resell intelligence at a
markup.
