# context rot

The degradation of an LLM's effective attention as its context window fills: unlike RAM, whose access is uniform and exact-by-address, context is content-addressed and its retrieval reliability drops with occupancy. Canonically captured in this bundle by the Chroma research reference — see [context rot — Chroma research](/knowledge/SWE/agentic/context-engineering/context-rot-chroma-research.md).
