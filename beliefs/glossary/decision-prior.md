# decision prior

A proposition an agent or person holds *true enough to guide action*, even when it is uncertain, subjective, or value-laden — the functional role beliefs play in a knowledge system: two reasoners with identical facts diverge in strategy because they hold different priors. Distinct from a fact (externally verifiable) and from [doctrine](/beliefs/glossary/doctrine.md) (a standing direction rather than a held proposition — see the [epistemic/teleological split](/beliefs/glossary/epistemic-teleological-split.md)). The proposed `belief` type in the [belief-layer plan](/meta/plans/belief-type-and-beliefs-namespace.md) would give operator-held decision priors a first-class home in this bundle.
