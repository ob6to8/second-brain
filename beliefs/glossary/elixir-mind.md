# elixir-mind

The ratified new name for **this brain itself**: per the accepted
[rename plan](/meta/plans/rename-second-brain-to-elixir-mind.md), the
`second-brain` repository, its Elixir app/module namespace, and the stable-id
prefix (`sb:` → `em:`) all move to **elixir-mind**. The name marries
[second mind](/beliefs/glossary/second-mind.md) (an AI layer that reasons over
stored knowledge, not just stores it) with the Elixir substrate the brain's
tooling is written in. The term's path: first applied and reverted as a
document rename (the 2026-07-13 taxonomy session), then ratified as the
project's name in the rename plan (accepted 2026-07-13), while the parallel
2026-07-14 AST/macros session used it for the narrower **prospective owned
runtime** — the tier-1/2 resident BEAM system mapped in
[the BEAM/Jido evaluation](/meta/analysis/beam-deployment-and-jido-2-evaluation.md),
i.e. the future mechanical plane *of* the elixir-mind project, whose design
constraints [the AST/macro analysis](/meta/analysis/elixir-ast-macros-for-llm-harnesses.md)
derives. The project-name sense is the ratified one; the runtime sense is a
part named after its whole.
