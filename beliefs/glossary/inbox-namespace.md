# inbox namespace

An item leaves the waiting room only by graduating into the bundle via `/intake`. Governed by the [`/research` skill](/.claude/skills/research/SKILL.md); see also [non-bundle namespace](/beliefs/glossary/non-bundle-namespace.md). Its durable, topical sibling is the [survey tier](/beliefs/glossary/survey-tier.md) (`survey/` bookmark registers) — same non-bundle mechanics, but staged by topic rather than dated.
