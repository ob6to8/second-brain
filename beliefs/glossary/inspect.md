# Inspect

Published as `inspect_ai` by a government body with no commercial stake in results, which is where its neutrality credential comes from: rerunning someone's eval under it requires trusting none of their harness code. Its logs cover the run-record layer of an eval ledger well but carry no [adjudication](/beliefs/glossary/adjudication.md) provenance or id-keyed label lineage — the division of labor this bundle's analysis recommends is ledger-authored [gold sets](/beliefs/glossary/gold-set.md) compiled into Inspect datasets for execution.
