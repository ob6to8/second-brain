# mental model

Two facets travel together and a usable model needs both: the **spatial** map
(*what the parts are and how they connect*) and the **causal/temporal** narrative
(*why it came to be this and not the alternative that was rejected*). Hand-writing
software forces the model into existence as a side effect — you cannot type the
next line without holding the shape in your head — but agent generation severs that
coupling, so the model no longer forms for free and must be produced deliberately.
In this brain that production is the reason the capture, routing-ledger, and
route-tag machinery exists; the standing intention is stated in
[comprehension of generated code](/meta/doctrine/comprehension-of-generated-code.md).
