# observer effect

In human-subject observation the leak is often incidental signal — an observer's keystroke bursts cue a participant about which moments matter, where silent pencil strokes do not. Distinct from experimenter bias (the recorder's expectations shaping what gets written down): here the *subject* shifts, not the record.
