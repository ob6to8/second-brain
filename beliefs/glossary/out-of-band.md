# out-of-band

For observation of agents the property does double duty: the notes survive the subject's crashes, and the examinee cannot read the examiner's running commentary (an agent can open any file on its machine; it cannot open a legal pad). A transcript written to the machine under test has moved back *in-band* — inside the subject's reach — regardless of what produced it.
