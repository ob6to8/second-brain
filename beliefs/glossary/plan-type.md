# plan (type)

The controlled `type` for intended work on the brain or its tooling: a design/decision record capturing motivation, the shape of the change, scope boundaries, and open questions, carrying a `status` (`proposed`/`accepted`/`in-progress`/`done`/`superseded`). Distinct from an `issue` (a problem) and a `methodology` (a repeatable how-to). Defined in the [controlled-type vocabulary](/meta/policy/controlled-type-vocabulary.md).
