# second mind

An AI layer over a personal knowledge store that doesn't just store information but reasons with it — synthesizing, connecting, and participating in judgment — the emerging contrast term to *second brain* (Tiago Forte's external repository of notes, whose purpose is storage and retrieval): a second brain remembers, a second mind thinks with you. The shift matters architecturally: a second mind needs explicit [decision priors](/beliefs/glossary/decision-prior.md), [doctrine](/beliefs/glossary/doctrine.md), and an externalized epistemology, not just organized notes.
