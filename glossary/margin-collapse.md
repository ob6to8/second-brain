# margin collapse

The thesis that frontier labs' ~90% inference gross margins are structurally exposed once open-weight models match "good enough" quality at a fraction of the price and switching between providers is near-frictionless. Canonically treated in the brain by the filed reference [GLM-5.2 and the coming AI margin collapse](/ai-industry/ai-margin-collapse-glm-5-2.md) (`sb:07610c`); actively contested by counter-frames (open/closed models on *different exponentials*; inference being *obviously profitable* for pure providers). This is a pointer entry — see that concept for the full argument and its rebuttals.
