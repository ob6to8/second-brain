# recall

In information retrieval, the fraction of relevant items that a search actually surfaces for a query. Low recall means existing relevant material is missed — the failure mode that matters for [dedup](/glossary/deduplication.md) at intake time, where a missed near-duplicate becomes a fragmented concept.
