# todo (type)

The controlled `type` for a lightweight actionable task — a single thing to be done, tracked until finished, carrying a `status` (`open`/`done`/`cancelled`) and filed under `meta/todos/` with the `/todo` skill. Distinct from an [issue](/glossary/issue-type.md) (a *problem* to diagnose and track) and a [plan](/glossary/plan-type.md) (a *design/decision record*): a todo is a plain *task to complete*. The two may defer to each other by cross-link — e.g. an issue's resolution criteria handing a strand to a todo. Defined in the [controlled-type vocabulary](/meta/policy/controlled-type-vocabulary.md).
