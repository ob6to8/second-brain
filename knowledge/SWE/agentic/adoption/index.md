# Adoption

How organizations adopt agentic coding — maturity models, rollout stages, and the
organizational bottlenecks and guardrails at each stage.

## Documents

- [Steps of AI Adoption (Boris Cherny)](/knowledge/SWE/agentic/adoption/steps-of-ai-adoption.md) — five-step maturity model for organizational AI-coding adoption, from Gated (0) to AI-native (4), with per-step agent counts, bottlenecks, products, and guardrails.
- [StrongDM's software factory (Simon Willison)](/knowledge/SWE/agentic/adoption/strongdm-software-factory.md) — a lived step-4 case study: a three-person team runs a pipeline where humans neither write nor review code, with holdout scenarios, agent-built digital twins, and probabilistic satisfaction metrics as the acceptance oracle in place of human review.
