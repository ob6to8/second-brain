# AI industry

Market, economic, and competitive analysis of the AI industry — valuations, ROI
timelines, margins, and competitive dynamics between labs and open-weight
alternatives. Distinct from [SWE](/knowledge/SWE/index.md), which is about *building*
software with or for AI; this domain is about the *business* of AI.

## References

- [The AI ROI runway could be long outside the tech sector (Torsten Slok / Apollo)](/knowledge/ai-industry/ai-roi-runway-outside-tech-sector.md) — AI valuations assume margin expansion that isn't showing up outside tech; most sectors face structural adoption barriers. `sb:2867ac` _(reference)_
- [GLM-5.2 and the coming AI margin collapse (Martin Alderson)](/knowledge/ai-industry/ai-margin-collapse-glm-5-2.md) — frontier labs' ~90% inference margins are exposed by open-weight models matching quality at a fraction of the price with frictionless switching. `sb:07610c` _(reference)_
