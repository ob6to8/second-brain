# Verification flows

Walkthroughs for **running and verifying** each of the brain's multi-step flows —
who does what (operator vs agent), how to invoke it, and exactly what to check to
confirm it executed correctly. Distinct from `meta/tutorials/` (the *why* behind
the tooling) and the policies (the *rules*): these are hands-on *did-it-work?*
checklists.
